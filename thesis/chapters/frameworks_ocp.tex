\section{GPOPS-II}
	GPOPS-II \cite{Patterson2014} is perhaps the most advaced tool for otimal control, it was developed ad University of Florida by Michael patterson and Anil V. Rao. other tools allows defininng the problem, but GPOPS-II is a level higher. It also includes advanced mesh refinement strategies, multiple phase OCP and automatic scaling. It interaces with MATLAB and C++, uses ADigator \cite{weinstein2017algorithm} for automatic differetnation. This tool is commercial and closed source. Implementaion in this thesis is heavily based on work of community from University of Florida.
