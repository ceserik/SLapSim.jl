\section{Tire - road interface modelling}
Job of the tire model in this thesis is to calculate forces acting on tire from the relative velocity and angle between tire and road. Input of general tire model is 3 speeds and 3 angles, output is 3 forces. 

Most forces that govern vehicle movement are transferred by the tires. The tire is considered to be the most important system of a vehicle and it is notoriously difficult to model \cite{Yuki-2019}. Thus tires need to be modeled with great caution in mind. Not only are there many parameters, such as tire wear, tire temperature, road roughness, road wetness, tire pressure, etc., that affect the resultant forces, they are also constantly changing during the lap. Therefore they are difficult to measure and model precisely \cite{ciglanskyAnalysisVehicleComponents}.
